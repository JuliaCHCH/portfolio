About Me

Dr. Chung Chee Hae

Management Consultant | Political Scientist | Policy Analyst

Chee Hae is an experienced management consultant with a diverse background in business administration, political science, and policy administration. Her extraordinary career path shows her go-getter attitude backed up with exclusive problem solving and people skills, which allowed her to experience every top-ranked fields that she had wished to participate in. She has a broad, yet in-depth range of working knowledge in management consulting, election studies(voting behavior analysis), and the government policy-making process. She is currently interested in innovative businesses’ roles in changing the market dynamics and the government’s policy decision-making process.
